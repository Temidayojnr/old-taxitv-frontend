ABOUT US

What is TaxiTV?

TaxiTV is an in-ride entertainment system built for riders to enjoy top quality content while on the move. TaxiTV is implemented with detachable screens (Android tablets) attached to the headrests in cars, content is then displayed on the screens for riders. Content will also be replaced over the internet as often as every two days so as to have fresh content for riders.

Who pays for the screens?

We do not provide the screens, the drivers/fleet management company would have to provide the tablets, we install the app and provide the tablet holder for the cars for FREE!

What does the company/driver benefit from this?

Your company/driver would make up to N10,000 per week from Adverts placed on the tablets during their rides.
